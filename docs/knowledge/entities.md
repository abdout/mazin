# Port Sudan Customs Clearance Entities

> Key parties, authorities, and organizations involved in the customs clearance process at Port Sudan.

## Overview

The customs clearance ecosystem involves **15+ distinct entities** across 6 categories:
1. Government Authorities
2. Port Authorities
3. Shipping Lines & Agents
4. Clearing Agents
5. Banks & Financial Institutions
6. Clients (Importers/Consignees)

---

## 1. Government Authorities

### Sudan Customs (الجمارك السودانية)

**Role**: Primary authority for import/export regulation and duty collection

**System**: ASYCUDA World (UNCTAD)

**Offices at Port Sudan**:
| Code | Name | Arabic |
|------|------|--------|
| PZUS0 | PortSudan South Quay | الميناء الجنوبي - الرصيف الجنوبي |
| PZUS1 | PortSudan South Quay (DAMADAMA) | الميناء الجنوبي - دمدمة |

**Functions**:
- Import/export declarations (IM4 forms)
- Duty assessment and collection
- Physical inspection of goods
- Customs laboratory testing
- Release authorization

**Documents Issued**:
- ASYCUDA Declaration (IM4)
- Assessment Notice
- Payment Receipt

---

### Ministry of Finance (وزارة المالية والتخطيط الاقتصادي)

**Role**: Revenue collection and financial oversight

**Sub-units**:
- Customs Headquarters (مقر الجمارك)
- Administrative Units (الوحدات الإدارية)

**Functions**:
- Customs duty receipt issuance
- Government fee collection
- Financial form processing (Fin Form 15)

**Documents Issued**:
- Ministry of Finance Receipt (Fin Form 15)
- Customs Payment Receipt

---

### SSMO (الهيئة السودانية للمواصفات والمقاييس)

**Full Name**: Sudanese Standards and Metrology Organization

**Arabic**: الهيئة السودانية للمواصفات والمقاييس

**Authority**: Ministry of Cabinet Affairs (وزارة مجلس الوزراء)

**Role**: Quality standards and product certification

**Functions**:
- Pre-shipment inspection coordination
- Laboratory testing
- Certificate of Conformity issuance
- Release authorization for regulated products

**Products Requiring SSMO**:
- Food and beverages
- Chemicals (glycerin, glycerol, petroleum jelly)
- Construction materials
- Vehicles and automotive parts
- Textiles and garments
- Electronics and electrical equipment
- Pharmaceuticals
- Cosmetics

**Documents Issued**:
- SSMO Release Form (استمارة خطاب إفراج صادر/وارد)
- Certificate of Conformity (شهادة مطابقة)

**Form Reference Format**: FR + 7 digits (e.g., FR2513494)

**Contact**: Through Ministry of Finance for payment

---

### Ministry of Trade (وزارة التجارة)

**Role**: Trade regulation and commerce oversight

**Functions**:
- Trade license verification
- Import permit coordination
- Commercial invoice verification

## 2. Port Authorities

### Sea Ports Corporation (هيئة الموانئ البحرية)

**Full Name**: Sea Ports Corporation - Sudan

**Location**: Port Sudan South Port Containers Terminal (الميناء الجنوبي - محطة الحاويات)

**Role**: Terminal operations and port services

**Functions**:
- Container handling and storage
- Vessel berthing coordination
- Equipment rental (cranes, forklifts)
- Gate operations
- Billing for port services

**Documents Issued**:

## Data Model Recommendations

```prisma
enum EntityType {
  CUSTOMS_OFFICE
  PORT_AUTHORITY
  SHIPPING_LINE
  SHIPPING_AGENT
  CLEARING_AGENT
  BANK
  GOVERNMENT_AUTHORITY
  IMPORTER
  EXPORTER
}

model Entity {
  id              String      @id @default(cuid())
  type            EntityType
  name            String
  nameArabic      String?
  taxId           String?     @unique
  licenseNumber   String?
  address         String?
  phone           String?
  email           String?
  website         String?
  bankDetails     Json?       // SWIFT, account numbers, etc.

  // Relations
  shipmentsAsAgent    Shipment[]  @relation("ShipmentAgent")

}
```
